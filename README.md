# 🛒 BlinkIT Grocery Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI Dashboard** developed using the BlinkIT Grocery Sales dataset. The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into sales performance, outlet characteristics, product categories, customer ratings, and inventory visibility.

The primary goal of this project is to transform raw retail sales data into actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making and operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

To analyze BlinkIT grocery sales data and identify key business trends related to:

* Sales performance
* Outlet efficiency
* Product demand
* Customer preferences
* Inventory visibility

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs and make data-driven decisions to improve business performance.

## 💼 Business Problem Statement

BlinkIT operates multiple grocery outlets across different locations and outlet formats. Management requires a centralized analytical solution to monitor sales performance and understand factors influencing business growth.

The organization needs answers to critical business questions regarding product performance, outlet efficiency, customer preferences, and inventory management.

This dashboard helps convert raw sales data into meaningful insights for strategic planning and operational improvements.

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions

### Sales Analysis

1. What is the total sales generated across all outlets?
2. Which item categories contribute the most to revenue?
3. Which products generate the highest and lowest sales?

### Outlet Analysis

4. Which outlet size performs best?
5. How does outlet age impact sales?
6. Which location tier contributes the highest revenue?

### Product Analysis

7. How does item visibility affect sales?
8. Which fat content category is preferred by customers?
9. Which product categories need inventory optimization?

### Customer Insights

10. How do customer ratings vary across outlet locations?
11. Which outlet types provide better customer satisfaction?

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Medium-sized outlets contribute the highest share of overall sales.
* Low-fat products generate a larger percentage of sales compared to regular-fat products.
* Tier-based outlet locations show noticeable differences in performance.
* Fruits, vegetables, and snack foods are among the top-performing categories.
* Product visibility significantly impacts sales performance.
* Older outlets tend to maintain more stable sales patterns.
